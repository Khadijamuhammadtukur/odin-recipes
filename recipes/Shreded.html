Shredded Chicken Soup

Description

This shredded chicken soup is a warm, comforting dish packed with tender shredded chicken, vegetables, and rich flavorful broth. It is perfect for cold evenings or when you need something nourishing.

The combination of soft shredded chicken, fresh herbs, and hearty ingredients makes this soup both satisfying and healthy.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded chicken
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup sweet corn
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup black beans
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro (optional)

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat a pot over medium heat and sauté onion and garlic until soft.
  2. Add the diced tomatoes and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  3. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a gentle boil.
  4. Add shredded chicken, corn, and black beans.
  5. Simmer for 10–15 minutes to blend flavors.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot.
